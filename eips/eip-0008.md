## Abstract

This EIP defines the Phase One Genesis reward distribution standard for the Bit Brains protocol.

Phase One consists of five (5) epochs, each one (1) month in duration, for a total Phase One duration of five (5) months.

Genesis Participants for Phase One consist exclusively of **Genesis Paired Assets**, composed of:
- Genesis Brains (ERC-721, fixed supply 1,500) with paired Ethscription artifacts
- Pickle Punks (ERC-721, fixed supply 1,500) with paired Ethscription artifacts

A fixed total of 20,000,000 Brain Intelligence Token (BIT) is reserved for Phase One rewards and is distributed once, at Phase One close (end of Epoch 5), using ENS-routed, claim-based rewards enforced through zero-knowledge (ZK) proofs.

Eligibility is binary. A Genesis Paired Asset receives either the full Phase One Genesis Allocation or zero rewards. Partial rewards are not permitted.

Any rewards not distributed due to ineligibility are returned to the Rewards Reserve Treasury.

---

## Motivation

Phase One requires simple, auditable, and non-custodial reward rules that favor long-term participation and continuity.

This EIP:
- Establishes ENS as the canonical reward routing identity
- Requires continuous paired-asset staking and Proof of Care continuity for the entire phase
- Enforces eligibility using zero-knowledge proofs
- Prevents inflation under all participation outcomes
- Clearly defines forfeiture and treasury reversion rules
- Preserves a clean transition into later dual-asset protocol phases

---

## Scope

This EIP applies only to:
- Genesis Paired Assets derived from Genesis Brains and Pickle Punks
- Phase One rewards (5 epochs, 5 months)

This EIP does not define rewards for later phases, additional asset classes, Node operation, or token issuance mechanics.

---

## Definitions

**Phase One**  
The first reward phase of the protocol, consisting of five epochs totaling five months.

**Epoch**  
A one-month evaluation window within Phase One, indexed Epoch 1 through Epoch 5.

**Genesis Paired Asset**  
The canonical Phase One participation unit composed of:
1. One Genesis ERC-721 NFT (Brain or Pickle Punk), and  
2. Its corresponding Ethscription artifact.

Both components together form the sole qualifying unit for staking, Proof of Care evaluation, and reward eligibility.

**Ethscription Artifact**  
An immutable on-chain calldata provenance record paired one-to-one with a Genesis ERC-721 NFT.  
Ethscriptions do not receive rewards independently but are required to remain valid and in good standing for the Paired Asset to qualify.

---

## Eligibility Requirements (Normative)

To receive Phase One rewards, a Genesis Paired Asset MUST satisfy **all** of the following for the entire five-month Phase One duration:

1. **Continuous Paired-Asset Staking**  
   - The ERC-721 NFT MUST remain staked for all five epochs.  
   - The paired Ethscription artifact MUST remain concurrently valid and registered in protocol-defined good standing.  
   - Any unstaking, transfer, invalidation, or disassociation of either component permanently disqualifies the Paired Asset.

2. **Proof of Care Continuity**  
   - Proof of Care continuity MUST be maintained without interruption for all five epochs.  
   - Continuity is validated via protocol checks and ZK proofs.

3. **ENS-Bound Ownership**  
   - A deterministic ENS subdomain under `bitbrains.eth` MUST resolve to the wallet currently staking the Paired Asset.  
   - ENS resolution and ownership are validated via ZK proofs at Phase One close.

Failure to meet any requirement results in permanent disqualification.

---

## Reward Allocation (Normative)

Total Phase One Reward Pool: **20,000,000 BIT**  
Total Genesis Paired Assets: **3,000**

Phase One Genesis Allocation per eligible Paired Asset:

20,000,000 BIT ÷ 3,000 = **6,666.666666 BIT**

Each Genesis Paired Asset that remains fully compliant across all five epochs receives exactly **6,666.666666 BIT** at Phase One close.

Rewards are not distributed per epoch and are settled only once, after Epoch 5.

---

## Disqualification and Forfeiture

Genesis Paired Assets that fail eligibility requirements at any point receive **zero rewards**.

Rewards allocated to disqualified or non-participating Paired Assets are **not redistributed** to remaining participants.

---

## Treasury Reversion

Any portion of the Phase One reward pool not distributed due to disqualification, non-participation, or failed claims MUST be returned to the **Rewards Reserve Treasury** at Phase One close.

These funds MUST NOT be routed to the Team Treasury and remain reserved for future protocol rewards.

---

## Summary

Phase One consists of five one-month epochs totaling five months.  
Only Genesis Paired Assets participate.  
100% paired-asset staking and 100% Proof of Care continuity are mandatory.  
Eligibility is binary: **6,666.666666 BIT or zero**.  
Unstaking, transfer, or paired-asset invalidation exits the protocol.  
Unused rewards return to the Rewards Reserve Treasury.
