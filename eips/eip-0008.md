## Abstract

This EIP defines the **Epoch 1 reward distribution standard** for **Genesis Participants**
within the Bit Brains protocol.

Genesis Participants consist of:
- **Genesis Brains** (ERC-721, 1,500 supply), and
- **Pickle Punks** (Ethscriptions, 1,500 supply).

Epoch 1 distributes a fixed total of **20,000,000 Brain Intelligence Token (BIT)** using
**ENS-routed, claim-based rewards** enforced via **zero-knowledge (ZK) eligibility proofs**.

Both Brains and Pickle Punks participate under the **same staking and Proof of Care (PoC)
continuity framework**, with identical qualification logic. Token emissions remain
strictly capped and independent of asset count.

All undistributed rewards are conserved in a dedicated **Rewards Reserve Treasury** and
are never routed to the Team Treasury.

---

## Motivation

Genesis participants require clear, auditable, and non-custodial reward rules.

This EIP:
- Establishes **ENS as the canonical reward routing identity**
- Applies **identical staking and PoC logic** to all Genesis assets
- Uses **ZK proofs** to preserve privacy
- Prevents inflation regardless of participant count
- Explicitly defines the destination of undistributed rewards

---

## Scope

This EIP applies **only** to:
- **Genesis Brains**
- **Pickle Punks (Ethscriptions)**
- **Epoch 1 rewards**

This EIP does **not** define:
- Rewards for Cerebrals or Brainiacs
- Reward logic for future epochs
- Token issuance or exchange mechanics

## Definitions

- **Genesis Participant**  
  A protocol-recognized asset introduced at Genesis (Brain or Pickle Punk).

- **Brain**  
  A Genesis Brain ERC-721 NFT from the fixed supply of 1,500.

- **Pickle Punk**  
  A Genesis Ethscription representing a stakable participation primitive.

- **Epoch**  
  A fixed reward evaluation window with a defined pool and ruleset.  
  Epoch 1 reward pool: **20,000,000 BIT**.

- **Staking**  
  Protocol-defined staking state requiring the Genesis Participant to remain continuously
  locked in the designated staking context for the required window.

- **Proof of Care (PoC) Continuity**  
  Protocol-defined continuity state validated via ZK proofs and/or protocol checks.

- **ENS Identity**  
  A deterministic ENS subdomain under `bitbrains.eth` resolving to a wallet controlled by
  the participant.

- **Rewards Reserve Treasury**  
  A dedicated treasury address used exclusively for future participant rewards.

## Reward Qualification Logic (Normative)

Each Genesis Participant is evaluated independently against two requirements:

- **Requirement A:** Staking  
- **Requirement B:** Proof of Care continuity  

Reward outcomes per asset:

| Staking | PoC Continuity | Reward Outcome |
|--------|---------------|----------------|
| true   | true          | 100%           |
| true   | false         | 50%            |
| false  | true          | 50%            |
| false  | false         | 0%             |
