## Abstract

This EIP defines the Phase One Genesis reward distribution standard for the Bit Brains protocol.

Phase One consists of five (5) epochs, each one (1) month in duration, for a total Phase One duration of five (5) months.

Genesis Participants for Phase One consist exclusively of:
- Genesis Brains (ERC-721, fixed supply 1,500)
- Pickle Punks (ERC-721, fixed supply 1,500)

A fixed total of 20,000,000 Brain Intelligence Token (BIT) is reserved for Phase One rewards and is distributed once, at Phase One close (end of Epoch 5), using ENS-routed, claim-based rewards enforced through zero-knowledge (ZK) proofs.

Eligibility is binary. A Genesis Participant receives either the full Phase One Genesis Allocation or zero rewards. Partial rewards are not permitted.

Any rewards not distributed due to ineligibility are returned to the Rewards Reserve Treasury.

---

## Motivation

Phase One requires simple, auditable, and non-custodial reward rules that favor long-term participation and continuity.

This EIP:
- Establishes ENS as the canonical reward routing identity
- Requires continuous staking and Proof of Care continuity for the entire phase
- Enforces eligibility using ZK proofs
- Prevents inflation under all participation outcomes
- Clearly defines forfeiture and treasury reversion rules
- Clarifies that Ethscriptions do not participate in Phase One rewards

---

## Scope

This EIP applies only to:
- Genesis Brains (ERC-721)
- Pickle Punks (ERC-721)
- Phase One rewards (5 epochs, 5 months)

This EIP does not define rewards for later phases, additional asset classes, or token issuance mechanics.

---

## Definitions

Phase One  
The first reward phase of the protocol, consisting of five epochs totaling five months.

Epoch  
A one-month evaluation window within Phase One, indexed Epoch 1 through Epoch 5.

Genesis Participant  
A Phase One-eligible ERC-721 Genesis Brain or Pickle Punk.

Ethscription  
An immutable on-chain provenance record. Ethscriptions do not stake and do not receive Phase One rewards. Ethscriptions are reserved for a later protocol rail after the initial phases.

---

## Eligibility Requirements (Normative)

To receive Phase One rewards, a Genesis Participant MUST satisfy all of the following for the entire five-month Phase One duration:

1. Continuous Staking  
The ERC-721 asset must remain staked for all five epochs. Any unstaking, transfer, or sale permanently disqualifies the asset.

2. Proof of Care Continuity  
Proof of Care continuity must be maintained without interruption and is validated via protocol checks and ZK proofs.

3. ENS-Bound Ownership  
A deterministic ENS subdomain under `bitbrains.eth` must resolve to the wallet currently staking the asset. Ownership and resolution are validated via ZK proofs at Phase One close.

Failure to meet any requirement results in permanent disqualification.

---

## Reward Allocation (Normative)

Total Phase One Reward Pool: 20,000,000 BIT  
Total Genesis Participants: 3,000 ERC-721 NFTs

Phase One Genesis Allocation per eligible NFT:

20,000,000 BIT ÷ 3,000 NFTs = 6,666.666666 BIT

Each Genesis Participant that remains fully compliant across all five epochs receives exactly 6,666.666666 BIT at Phase One close.

Rewards are not distributed per epoch and are settled only once, after Epoch 5.

---

## Disqualification and Forfeiture

Genesis Participants that fail eligibility requirements at any point receive zero rewards.

Rewards allocated to disqualified or non-participating assets are not redistributed to remaining participants.

---

## Treasury Reversion

Any portion of the Phase One reward pool not distributed due to disqualification, non-participation, or failed claims MUST be returned to the Rewards Reserve Treasury at Phase One close.

These funds MUST NOT be routed to the Team Treasury and remain reserved for future protocol rewards.

---

## Summary

Phase One consists of five one-month epochs totaling five months.  
Only ERC-721 Genesis Brains and Pickle Punks participate.  
100% staking and 100% Proof of Care continuity are mandatory.  
Eligibility is binary: 6,666.666666 BIT or zero.  
Selling or unstaking exits the protocol.  
Unused rewards return to the Rewards Reserve Treasury.
