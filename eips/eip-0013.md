# Ethereum Node Development DAO (END DAO)
## Constitutional Governance & Revenue Framework

Launch Date: March 1, 2026  
DAO Activation Date: March 1, 2027  

---

# 1. Introduction

The Ethereum Node Development DAO (END DAO) is the constitutional governance body of the Bit Brains and BITY Nodes ecosystem.

END DAO governs:

- Token emissions  
- Treasury operations  
- Revenue allocation mechanisms  
- Node reward distribution  
- Constitutional guardrails  
- Long-term economic sustainability  

The DAO becomes fully operational on March 1, 2027, one year after protocol launch.

---

# 2. Governance Activation Timeline

## Phase 1 — Pre-DAO Year  
(March 1, 2026 – March 1, 2027)

During the first operational year:

- Governance is not yet active.
- Founders retain operational control.
- Infrastructure, audits, and core systems are stabilized.

### Revenue Allocation (Year 1)

- 50% → Founders  
- 50% → Node Fund (Treasury Reserve)  

---

## Phase 2 — DAO Operational  
(Beginning March 1, 2027)

On March 1, 2027:

- END DAO becomes fully operational.
- Governance proposals may be submitted and voted upon.
- Emission governance activates.
- Treasury governance activates.

### Revenue Allocation (Post-Activation)

- 70% → Node Fund (DAO Treasury)  
- 30% → Founders  

---

# 3. Revenue Sources

Protocol revenue includes:

- Brains-as-a-Service (BaaS)  
- Software-as-a-Service (SaaS)  
- Nodes-as-a-Service (NaaS)  
- Infrastructure licensing  
- Analytics services  
- Protocol-native utilities  
- Future marketplace or exchange infrastructure (if deployed)  

Revenue is generated through infrastructure and service usage, not through token transfer taxation.

# 5. NFT Revenue & Royalty Routing

All financial gain derived from NFT ecosystems, including:

- Primary mint sales  
- Secondary market royalties  
- NFT licensing revenue  

Shall compensate:

- Founders  
- Contributing Artists  

NFT revenue is separate from token emission economics.

NFT-related revenue does not alter:

- Token supply  
- Emission schedules  
- Treasury allocation percentages  

NFT intellectual property compensation is constitutionally protected and remains outside DAO discretionary treasury governance.

---

# 6. Service-Issued NFTs & ENS Delegation

Brains-as-a-Service (BaaS), Nodes-as-a-Service (NaaS), and Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) may issue NFTs representing:

- Service participation  
- Node operation credentials  
- Intelligence infrastructure rights  

These service NFTs may be delegated:

- bitynodes.eth subdomains  
- Future protocol ENS namespaces  

ENS routing enables future reward routing, identity continuity, and governance participation.

---

# 7. Native Token — BITS

The native token of the ecosystem is:

**BITS — Brain Intelligence Technology Services**

BITS functions as:

- The primary reward emission token  
- The governance participation token  
- The infrastructure utility token  
- The economic coordination mechanism of the ecosystem  

BITS powers:

- Brains-as-a-Service (BaaS)  
- Software-as-a-Service (SaaS)  
- Nodes-as-a-Service (NaaS)  
- Node reward distribution  
- Governance voting within END DAO  
- Future service-layer execution and infrastructure utilities  

---

## 7.1 Fixed Supply

Total Supply: **1,000,000,000 BITS** (Fixed Cap)

The total supply is permanently capped and cannot be increased.

---

## 7.2 Allocation Structure

- 20% → Founders  
- 10% → Year-1 Continuity Airdrop  
- 20% → Market Allocation  
- 50% → Node Reward Allocation (Emission-Based)  

These allocation percentages are constitutional parameters.

---

## 7.3 Emission Model

Node Reward Allocation: 500,000,000 BITS

Emission Structure:

- 40-year linear emission  
- 12,500,000 BITS per year  
- 1.25% of total supply annually  
- 2.5% of reward allocation annually  

Emission cannot exceed supply cap.

Governance may reduce emission rate but may not increase beyond constitutional boundaries without Tier-3 approval.

---

# 8. Supermajority Governance Structure

Tier 1 — Operational Decisions  
70% Approval Required

Tier 2 — Treasury & Economic Decisions  
70% Approval Required

Tier 3 — Constitutional Decisions  
75%+ Approval Required

Certain constraints may be permanently immutable.

---

# 9. Constitutional Guardrails

The following are structural invariants:

- Fixed total supply  
- Allocation percentages  
- Emission cap boundaries  
- Founders revenue protections  
- NFT intellectual property compensation structure  

Governance evolves the ecosystem but does not rewrite genesis-level constraints without supermajority approval.

---

# 10. Economic Balance Model

The system operates through:

- Predictable emission (40-year linear release)  
- Service-based revenue (BaaS / SaaS / NaaS)  
- NFT ecosystem monetization  
- Treasury-controlled capital deployment  
- Governance oversight  

This creates a long-term, infrastructure-first economic model.
