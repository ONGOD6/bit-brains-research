## Abstract

This EIP defines eligibility, allocation, and reward rules for Brainiac NFTs
introduced in Phase 3 of the Bit Brains protocol.

Brainiac eligibility is determined by sustained compliance across Cerebral NFTs,
including staking and Proof of Care (PoC) continuity. Additional eligibility tiers
may incorporate Genesis Assets as optional inputs to Phase 3 evaluation.

All eligibility enforcement and reward routing is performed using ENS-based identity
and zero-knowledge (ZK) verification.

---

## Scope

This EIP applies exclusively to:
- Brainiac NFTs introduced in Phase 3
- Phase 3 eligibility determination

This EIP does not modify:
- Epoch 1 reward rules (EIP-0008)
- Phase 2 Cerebral rules (EIP-0009)
- Token supply or emissions (EIP-0003)
- AIT execution constraints (EIP-0007)

---

## Definitions

- Brainiac
  A Phase 3 ERC-721 NFT governed by this EIP.

- Cerebral
  A Phase 2 ERC-721 NFT governed by EIP-0009.
  Cerebrals are acquired only through the Phase 2 public mint purchase mechanism.
  No Cerebrals are free, guaranteed, or claimable by Genesis asset ownership.

- Genesis Asset
  A protocol-recognized Genesis asset, including:
  - Genesis Brains, and
  - Pickle Punks (Ethscriptions).

- Epoch
  A fixed reward accounting period.

- Staking
  Protocol-defined staking state requiring continuous participation.

- Proof of Care (PoC) Continuity
  Protocol-defined continuity state validated via ZK proofs.

- Secondary Rewards Treasury
  A dedicated treasury holding forfeited assets and undistributed rewards,
  reserved exclusively for future participant rewards.

- ENS-Resolved Wallet
  The wallet returned by resolving the participant’s canonical ENS identity.

## Phase 3 Brainiac Eligibility Overview

Eligibility to claim Brainiac NFTs depends on continuous staking and PoC compliance
across Cerebral NFTs and, where applicable, optional Genesis Assets associated with
the participant’s continuity history.

All eligibility outcomes are evaluated deterministically and enforced at claim time.

---

## Cerebral-Only Eligibility

### Full Cerebral Compliance (100%)

A Cerebral holder is fully compliant if:
- the Cerebral NFT(s) are staked, AND
- PoC continuity is maintained.

Entitlement:
- Eligible to claim two (2) Brainiac NFTs.

---

### Partial Cerebral Compliance (50%)

A Cerebral holder is partially compliant if exactly one requirement is satisfied:
- staking without PoC, OR
- PoC without staking.

Entitlement:
- Eligible to claim one (1) Brainiac NFT.

---

## Genesis + Cerebral Combined Eligibility (Maximum Tier)

A participant who satisfies all of the following:
- Stakes one (1) Genesis Asset (Genesis Brain or Pickle Punk),
- Stakes two (2) Cerebral NFTs, AND
- Maintains PoC continuity across all staked assets,

is eligible to claim:
- three (3) Brainiac NFTs.

This represents the highest eligibility tier for Phase 3.

---

## Protocol-Owned Assets

Any Brainiac NFTs claimed by the protocol:
- SHALL remain staked under protocol control,
- SHALL follow identical reward rules,
- SHALL route all rewards to the Secondary Rewards Treasury.

No preferential treatment is permitted.

---

## Exit and Forfeiture Rules

If a participant:
- unstakes required assets,
- breaks PoC continuity,
- transfers required assets out of the ENS-resolved wallet, or
- otherwise exits the protocol,

then:
- all pending rewards are forfeited, and
- all unclaimed Brainiac entitlements are forfeited.

---

## Forfeited Brainiac NFTs

Forfeited Brainiac NFTs:
- SHALL be routed to the Secondary Rewards Treasury,
- SHALL remain staked under protocol control,
- SHALL accrue rewards for future participant incentives.

These assets:
- SHALL NOT default to the Team Treasury.

---

## Reward Enforcement

All Brainiac eligibility and reward claims are enforced via:
- ENS resolution, and
- zero-knowledge (ZK) verification.

No discretionary overrides are permitted.
NFT custody is never required.

---

## Hypothetical Supply Disclosure

The total Brainiac supply is not fixed at this stage.

Final supply depends on:
- Cerebral compliance outcomes,
- Genesis + Cerebral combined compliance,
- forfeitures during Phase 2 and Phase 3.

All quantities prior to Phase 2 close are explicitly hypothetical.

A reconciliation report:
- MUST be published before Phase 3 activation.

---

## Non-Guarantee

- Reward rates are not fixed or perpetual.
- Parameters may be reassessed post-AIT.
- Total token supply SHALL NOT increase.
- Accrued rewards SHALL NOT be modified retroactively.

---

## Security and Economic Considerations

- Phase 3 introduces no new emissions
- All reward flows remain bounded
- ENS + ZK enforcement prevents manipulation
- Continuity requirements prevent short-term extraction

---

## Conclusion

This EIP defines Phase 3 Brainiac eligibility in a manner that:
- centers Cerebral compliance as the primary requirement,
- optionally incorporates Genesis Assets for maximum-tier outcomes,
- preserves economic discipline,
- enforces long-term continuity,
- and enables clean progression toward AIT states.
