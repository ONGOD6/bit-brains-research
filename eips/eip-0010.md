## Abstract

This EIP defines the Phase Three final reward distribution standard
for the Bit Brains protocol.

Phase Three is the final Genesis-era reward phase.

Phase Three consists of three (3) epochs.
Each epoch is one (1) month.
Total Phase Three duration is three (3) months.

Participation in Phase Three is restricted to **Brainiac Paired Assets**,
composed of:
- A Brainiac ERC-721 NFT, and
- Its corresponding Ethscription artifact.

Phase Three distributes a fixed total of
**10,000,000 Brain Intelligence Token (BIT)**.

Rewards are settled once, at Phase Three close,
after the end of Epoch 3.

Rewards are routed through ENS.
Eligibility is enforced using zero-knowledge (ZK) proofs.

Eligibility is binary.
A qualifying Brainiac Paired Asset receives full rewards or zero.
Partial rewards are not permitted.

Unused rewards return to the Rewards Reserve Treasury.

---

## Motivation

Phase Three finalizes Genesis-era rewards under the fully realized
dual-asset evolution model of the Bit Brains protocol.

This EIP:
- Applies the paired-asset eligibility rules established in EIP-0008 and EIP-0009
- Uses ENS as the canonical reward routing identity
- Requires continuous paired-asset staking
- Requires Proof of Care continuity
- Uses ZK proofs for verification
- Prevents inflation under all outcomes
- Defines final forfeiture and treasury reversion rules

---

## Scope

This EIP applies only to:
- Brainiac Paired Assets
- Phase Three rewards (3 epochs, 3 months)

This EIP does not define:
- Rewards for prior phases
- Post-Genesis reward programs
- Node-based incentives
- Token issuance or exchange mechanics

## Definitions

**Phase Three**  
The final Genesis reward phase of the protocol.
Consists of three epochs.
Total duration is three months.

**Epoch**  
A one-month evaluation window within Phase Three.

**Brainiac Paired Asset**  
The canonical Phase Three participation unit composed of:
1. One Brainiac ERC-721 NFT, and  
2. Its corresponding Ethscription artifact.

Both components together are required for staking, Proof of Care evaluation,
and reward eligibility.

**Proof of Care Continuity**  
Protocol-defined continuity requirements validated through ZK proofs
and protocol checks.

**ENS Identity**  
A deterministic ENS subdomain under `bitbrains.eth`
that resolves to the wallet staking the Brainiac Paired Asset.

**Rewards Reserve Treasury**  
A dedicated treasury address used exclusively for future participant rewards.

---

## Eligibility Requirements (Normative)

To receive Phase Three rewards, a Brainiac Paired Asset MUST satisfy
**all** requirements for the entire Phase Three duration:

1. **Continuous Paired-Asset Staking**  
   - The Brainiac ERC-721 NFT MUST remain staked for all three epochs.  
   - The paired Ethscription artifact MUST remain concurrently valid
     and registered in protocol-defined good standing.  
   - Any unstaking, transfer, invalidation, or disassociation of either
     component permanently disqualifies the Paired Asset.

2. **Proof of Care Continuity**  
   Proof of Care continuity MUST remain unbroken.
   Continuity is validated using ZK proofs.

3. **ENS-Bound Ownership**  
   The ENS subdomain MUST resolve to the staking wallet.
   Ownership and resolution are validated via ZK proofs at Phase Three close.

Failure of any requirement results in zero rewards.

## Phase Three Reward Allocation (Normative)

Total Phase Three reward pool:
**10,000,000 BIT**

Total participating Brainiac Paired Assets:
**4,000**

Per-Brainiac Paired Asset reward allocation:

10,000,000 BIT ÷ 4,000  
= **2,500 BIT**

Rewards are distributed once, at Phase Three close.
No per-epoch payouts are permitted.

---

## Disqualification and Forfeiture

Brainiac Paired Assets that fail eligibility requirements receive zero rewards.

Rewards allocated to disqualified or non-participating Paired Assets
are not redistributed.

---

## Treasury Reversion

Any portion of the Phase Three reward pool not distributed
MUST be returned to the Rewards Reserve Treasury.

Unused rewards MUST NOT be routed to the Team Treasury.
Funds remain reserved under protocol governance.
