## Abstract

This EIP defines minting conditions and reward rules for Brainiac NFTs introduced
in Phase 3 of the Bit Brains protocol.

Brainiac NFTs are obtained exclusively through a Phase 3 public mint purchase
mechanism. No participant receives free, guaranteed, or entitlement-based Brainiac
issuance.

Ownership of Genesis Brains, Pickle Punks, or Cerebrals does not confer preferential
access, allocation, or quantity in the Brainiac mint.

Brainiac NFTs follow the same per-asset dual-record model established in prior
phases: each Brainiac mint produces one ERC-721 NFT and one immutable Ethscription
committed to Ethereum transaction calldata.

Participation in prior phases affects reward eligibility and future Autonomous
Intelligent Technology (AIT) status only. It does not affect Brainiac mint access.

---

## Scope

This EIP applies exclusively to:
- Brainiac NFTs introduced in Phase 3
- Phase 3 minting and reward enforcement rules

This EIP does not modify:
- Phase 1 Brain rules (EIP-0008)
- Phase 2 Cerebral rules (EIP-0009)
- Token supply or emissions (EIP-0003)
- AIT execution constraints (EIP-0007)

## Definitions

Brainiac  
A Phase 3 ERC-721 NFT governed by this EIP.

Brainiac Ethscription  
An immutable calldata-based Ethscription committed at the time of Brainiac mint.
The Ethscription establishes a timestamped, verifiable truth linking the Brainiac
NFT to its protocol-recognized creation context without custody, wrapping, or
mutation.

Genesis Asset  
A protocol-recognized Genesis asset, including:
- Genesis Brains
- Pickle Punks

Cerebral  
A Phase 2 ERC-721 NFT governed by EIP-0009.

Epoch  
A fixed reward accounting period.

Staking  
Protocol-defined staking state for reward eligibility and continuity evaluation.

Proof of Care (PoC) Continuity  
Protocol-defined continuity state validated via zero-knowledge proofs.

Secondary Rewards Treasury  
A dedicated treasury holding forfeited and undistributed rewards, reserved
exclusively for future participant incentives.

ENS-Resolved Wallet  
The wallet returned by resolving the participant’s canonical ENS identity.

## Phase 3 Brainiac Supply

Phase 3 introduces Brainiac NFTs through a single public mint.

- Total supply: 4,000 Brainiacs

This completes the protocol’s initial target of 10,000 NFTs across all phases.

All Brainiacs follow identical minting, staking, Proof of Care, and reward rules,
regardless of minter identity or prior asset ownership.

---

## Phase 3 Public Mint

The Phase 3 Brainiac mint is a public sale.

- Any participant may purchase Brainiac NFTs
- No prior protocol asset ownership is required
- No preferential access, quantity, or allocation exists

The only requirements to mint are:
- Resolution of the protocol-defined ENS identity
- Successful transaction execution

---

## Brainiac Minting and Ethscription Commitment

Each Brainiac mint SHALL produce two protocol-recognized records:

1. One ERC-721 Brainiac NFT
2. One immutable Ethscription committed to Ethereum transaction calldata

Ethscriptions:
- are immutable once committed,
- do not alter ownership semantics,
- do not imply economic guarantees,
- MAY be referenced by future protocol advancements.

---

## Staking, Rewards, and AIT Progression

Brainiac NFTs MAY be staked and participate in Proof of Care under applicable
standards.

Staking and Proof of Care:
- affect reward eligibility only,
- affect future Autonomous Intelligent Technology (AIT) status,
- affect eligibility for ERC-8004 Autonomous Intelligence Technology Node and
  Real World Asset (RWA) pathways.

Staking and Proof of Care do NOT:
- grant additional Brainiac NFTs,
- alter mint pricing or quantity,
- create entitlements of any kind.

---

## Exit and Forfeiture Rules

If a Brainiac holder:
- unstakes,
- breaks Proof of Care continuity,
- breaks ENS resolution, or
- otherwise exits the protocol,

then pending rewards MAY be forfeited according to applicable reward standards.

The Brainiac NFT itself is not revoked or burned.

---

## Reward Enforcement

All reward eligibility and claims are enforced exclusively via:
- ENS resolution, and
- zero-knowledge verification.

No discretionary overrides are permitted.
NFT custody is never required.

---

## Non-Guarantee

- Reward rates are not fixed or perpetual
- Parameters may be reassessed post-AIT
- Total token supply SHALL NOT increase
- Accrued rewards SHALL NOT be modified retroactively

---

## Security and Economic Considerations

- Phase 3 introduces no new emissions
- Brainiac supply is fixed at 4,000
- ENS + ZK enforcement prevents manipulation
- Separation of mint access and rewards prevents protocol capture

---

## Conclusion

This EIP defines Phase 3 Brainiac minting as a fully public, non-entitlement-based
process consistent with Phase 1 and Phase 2 standards.

Participation in earlier phases affects rewards and future Autonomous Intelligent
Technology outcomes only, preserving fairness, economic discipline, and clean
progression toward ERC-8004 AIT Nodes and Real World Asset integration.
