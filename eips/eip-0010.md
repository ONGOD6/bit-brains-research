## Abstract

This EIP defines the Phase Three final reward distribution standard
for the Bit Brains protocol.

Phase Three is the final Genesis-era reward phase.

Participation in Phase Three is restricted to **Brainiac Paired Assets**,
composed of:
- a Brainiac ERC-721 NFT, and
- its corresponding Ethscription artifact.

Phase Three rewards are:
- treasury-funded,
- non-inflationary,
- settled once at Phase Three close,
- routed through ENS, and
- verified using zero-knowledge (ZK) proofs.

All reward amounts are **TO BE DETERMINED**.

Eligibility is binary.
A qualifying Brainiac Paired Asset receives rewards or zero.
Partial rewards are not permitted.

Unused rewards return to the Rewards Reserve Treasury.

## Motivation

Phase Three finalizes Genesis-era rewards under the fully realized
dual-asset evolution model of the Bit Brains protocol.

This EIP:
- applies paired-asset eligibility rules,
- uses ENS as the canonical reward routing identity,

## Definitions

**Phase Three**  
The final Genesis reward phase of the protocol.
Evaluation structure and duration are governance-defined.

**Brainiac Paired Asset**  
The canonical Phase Three participation unit composed of:
1. one Brainiac ERC-721 NFT, and  
2. its corresponding Ethscription artifact.

Both components are required for staking,
Proof of Care evaluation, and reward eligibility.

**Proof of Care Continuity**  
Protocol-defined continuity requirements validated
through ZK proofs and protocol checks.

**ENS Identity**  
A deterministic ENS subdomain under `bitbrains.eth`
that resolves to the wallet staking the Brainiac Paired Asset.

**Rewards Reserve Treasury**  
A dedicated treasury address used exclusively
for participant rewards.

---

## Eligibility Requirements (Normative)

To receive Phase Three rewards, a Brainiac Paired Asset MUST satisfy
**all** requirements for the entire Phase Three evaluation window:

1. **Continuous Paired-Asset Staking**  
   Both the Brainiac ERC-721 NFT and its paired Ethscription
   MUST remain valid and in protocol-defined good standing.
   Any unstaking, transfer, invalidation, or disassociation
   permanently disqualifies the Paired Asset.

2. **Proof of Care Continuity**  
   Proof of Care continuity MUST remain unbroken.
   Verified via ZK proofs.

3. **ENS-Bound Ownership**  
   The ENS subdomain MUST resolve to the staking wallet.
   Resolution is validated via ZK proofs at Phase Three close.

Failure of any requirement results in zero rewards.

---

## Phase Three Reward Authorization (Normative)

Phase Three rewards, if authorized, MUST satisfy all constraints below:

- **Funding Source:** Rewards Reserve Treasury only  
- **Supply:** Non-inflationary  
- **Total Allocation:** CAPPED (TO BE DETERMINED)  
- **Per-Asset Allocation:** TO BE DETERMINED  
- **Settlement Timing:** Single settlement at Phase Three close  

This EIP does not imply entitlement.
Rewards MAY be reduced, delayed, restructured, or set to zero.

---

## Settlement and Claims

Rewards are settled once, at Phase Three close.

Claims are:
- user-initiated,
- gas-paid by the user,
- routed through ENS, and
- verified using zero-knowledge proofs.

The protocol MUST NOT automatically push rewards.

---

## Disqualification and Forfeiture

Brainiac Paired Assets that fail eligibility requirements
receive zero rewards.

Rewards allocated to disqualified or non-participating
Paired Assets are not redistributed.

---

## Treasury Reversion

Any portion of the Phase Three reward allocation
not distributed MUST be returned to the Rewards Reserve Treasury.

Unused rewards MUST NOT be routed to the Team Treasury.
Funds remain under protocol governance control.
