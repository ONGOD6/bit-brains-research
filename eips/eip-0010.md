## Abstract

This EIP defines the Phase Three final reward distribution standard
for the Bit Brains protocol.

Phase Three is the final reward phase.

Phase Three consists of three (3) epochs.
Each epoch is one (1) month.
Total Phase Three duration is three (3) months.

Participating assets are Brainiacs.
Brainiacs are ERC-721 NFTs.

Phase Three distributes a fixed total of
10,000,000 Brain Intelligence Token (BIT).

Rewards are settled once, at Phase Three close.
Settlement occurs after the end of Epoch 3.

Rewards are routed through ENS.
Eligibility is enforced using zero-knowledge (ZK) proofs.

Eligibility is binary.
A Brainiac receives full rewards or zero.
Partial rewards are not permitted.

Unused rewards return to the Rewards Reserve Treasury.

---

## Motivation

Phase Three finalizes Genesis-era rewards.

This EIP:
- Applies the same rules as EIP-0008 and EIP-0009
- Uses ENS as the canonical reward identity
- Requires continuous staking
- Requires Proof of Care continuity
- Uses ZK proofs for verification
- Prevents inflation
- Defines final forfeiture and treasury reversion rules

---

## Scope

This EIP applies only to:
- Brainiacs (ERC-721)
- Phase Three rewards (3 epochs, 3 months)

This EIP does not define:
- Future phases
- Additional reward programs
- Token issuance or exchange mechanics

## Definitions

Phase Three  
The final reward phase of the protocol.
Consists of three epochs.
Total duration is three months.

Epoch  
A one-month evaluation window.

Brainiac  
A protocol-recognized ERC-721 NFT eligible for Phase Three rewards.

Proof of Care Continuity  
Protocol-defined continuity requirements.
Validated using ZK proofs and protocol checks.

ENS Identity  
A participant-chosen ENS subdomain under `bitbrains.eth`.
Must resolve to the wallet staking the Brainiac.

---

## Eligibility Requirements (Normative)

To receive Phase Three rewards, a Brainiac MUST
satisfy all requirements for the entire Phase Three duration:

1. Continuous Staking  
The Brainiac MUST remain staked for all three epochs.
Any unstaking, transfer, or sale permanently disqualifies the asset.

2. Proof of Care Continuity  
Proof of Care continuity MUST remain unbroken.
Validated using ZK proofs.

3. ENS-Bound Ownership  
The ENS subdomain MUST resolve to the staking wallet.
Ownership and resolution are validated via ZK proofs.

Failure of any requirement results in zero rewards.

---

## Phase Three Reward Allocation (Normative)

Total Phase Three reward pool:
10,000,000 BIT

Total participating Brainiacs:
4,000

Per-Brainiac reward allocation:

10,000,000 BIT ÷ 4,000 Brainiacs
= 2,500 BIT per eligible Brainiac

Rewards are distributed once.
Distribution occurs at Phase Three close.
No per-epoch payouts are permitted.

---

## Disqualification and Forfeiture

Brainiacs that fail eligibility requirements receive zero rewards.

Rewards allocated to disqualified Brainiacs are not redistributed.

---

## Treasury Reversion

Any portion of the Phase Three reward pool not distributed
MUST be returned to the Rewards Reserve Treasury.

Unused rewards MUST NOT be routed to the Team Treasury.
Funds remain reserved under protocol governance.
