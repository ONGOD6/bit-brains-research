## Abstract

This EIP defines the economic stewardship principles governing the Bit Brains protocol.
The framework prioritizes public good creation, long-term alignment, continuity, and
responsibility over extraction-driven or speculative economic models.

Economic value within Bit Brains is treated as a resource to be stewarded rather than
exploited.

This EIP recognizes **paired Genesis participation primitives**, composed of canonical
ERC-721 NFTs and their associated Ethscription artifacts, as valid contributors to
protocol stewardship when aligned through Proof of Care, continuity, and dual-asset
staking requirements.

Genesis assets are made fully knowable on-chain from inception through **dual
Ethereum-native pathways**: programmable token state and immutable calldata provenance.

---

## Motivation

Decentralized systems frequently fail due to misaligned incentives, short-term
extraction, and a lack of accountability over time.

Bit Brains introduces economic stewardship as a foundational principle to ensure that
economic participation remains aligned with public good outcomes, patience, and
long-term protocol health.

The protocol explicitly permits multiple Genesis asset forms while enforcing a **single,
unified standard of stewardship, accountability, and continuity**, independent of asset
representation.

---

## Canonical Genesis Reference

This EIP inherits and defers to the Bit Brains Genesis Parameters.

Genesis Parameters define the initial conditions of the protocol at inception.
They do not represent protocol invariants and may evolve through governance,
future EIPs, or community consensus without violating core protocol principles.

Nothing in this EIP guarantees economic rewards, governance authority, or evolutionary
progression.

---

## Dual-Asset Stewardship Invariant

Economic stewardship within the Bit Brains protocol is evaluated at the level of the
**Paired Asset**.

A Paired Asset consists of:
1. A canonical **ERC-721 Genesis asset** (Brain or Pickle Punk), and
2. Its associated **Ethscription artifact**, serving as immutable calldata provenance.

Economic participation, continuity evaluation, and eligibility for future protocol
capabilities apply **only** to the Paired Asset as a unified unit.

No ERC-721 asset or Ethscription artifact is considered independently qualifying for
stewardship recognition, continuity accrual, or economic influence.

---

## Principles of Economic Stewardship

### 1. Stewardship Over Ownership

Economic participation implies responsibility.
Control, influence, and recognition must be continuously earned through alignment and
care rather than possession alone.

### 2. Public-First Orientation

The protocol prioritizes broad participation and public benefit over privileged access,
concentrated extraction, or speculative advantage.

### 3. Patience as an Economic Signal

Time is a core economic dimension.
Value accrues to participants who demonstrate sustained alignment over extended
continuity windows.

### 4. Alignment Over Speculation

Economic mechanisms are designed to discourage short-term speculation and reward
long-term commitment, continuity, and care.

### 5. Renewable Influence

No economic influence is permanent.
Stewardship is continuously reassessed through participation, continuity, and
protocol-defined evaluation.

## Genesis Participation Primitives

The Bit Brains protocol recognizes multiple Genesis participation primitives, all of
which are evaluated under the same stewardship framework when paired and aligned.

Genesis participation primitives include:

- **Brains** — ERC-721 NFTs representing the original identity and participation unit
  of the protocol, each paired with an Ethscription artifact anchoring Genesis
  provenance in immutable Ethereum calldata.

- **Pickle Punks** — ERC-721 NFTs paired with Ethscription artifacts serving as
  immutable calldata-based Genesis participation records.

For all Genesis participation primitives:

- An Ethscription artifact MAY be minted in association with the ERC-721 asset
- Ethscriptions are immutable once created
- Ethscriptions possess independent hashes and provenance
- Ethscriptions do not replace ERC-721 NFTs
- Together, the ERC-721 asset and Ethscription artifact form a Paired Asset that
  represents Genesis participation

Genesis participation does not guarantee economic rewards, governance authority, or
evolutionary progression.

All influence, recognition, and advancement remain contingent on sustained Proof of
Care, continuity, dual-asset staking, and governance-defined thresholds.

## Protocol Emergence: Brain → Cerebral

This EIP defines the emergence of the protocol through two canonical participation
stages: Brain and Cerebral.

No additional stages are defined in this document.

Autonomy is not a stage.
It is a **capability** attained through Proof of Care, epochs, continuity, and
dual-asset alignment, and may be achieved by eligible Paired Assets under protocol
rules.

---

### Brain

A Brain is a Genesis ERC-721 asset paired with its Ethscription artifact.

Through protocol-defined epochs, Proof of Care, continuity, and dual-asset staking,
a Brain may achieve **Autonomous Intelligent Technology (AIT) capability**, as defined
in EIP-0011.

AIT capability:
- Operates under bounded autonomous execution constraints
- Exhibits protocol-recognized intelligent behavior
- Maintains cryptographic provenance and accountability
- May support Real World Asset (RWA) attribution

AIT capability:
- Does NOT create a new token
- Does NOT imply minting
- Does NOT automatically grant economic privilege

---

### Cerebral

Cerebral represents the highest participation state currently defined.

The Cerebral state reflects protocol-level cognitive and economic maturity, expressed
through sustained stewardship, continuity, and Proof of Care.

Cerebral:
- Builds on AIT-capable participation
- Represents long-term alignment and economic intelligence
- Remains subject to stewardship, continuity, and accountability
- Confers no permanent rights or guarantees

---

## Scope Limitation

This EIP intentionally defines only stewardship principles and participation states.

It does not define:
- Token supply or issuance mechanics
- Reward formulas or yield schedules
- Governance implementation details
- Technical enforcement mechanisms
- Evolutionary minting or settlement pathways

These elements are defined in subsequent EIPs.

---

## Rationale

By defining economic stewardship before mechanics or distribution, Bit Brains ensures
that future technical and economic decisions remain grounded in long-term alignment,
public good creation, and accountable participation — regardless of Genesis asset form.
