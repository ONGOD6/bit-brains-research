## Abstract

This EIP defines the Phase Two reward distribution standard for **Cerebral Paired Assets**
within the Bit Brains protocol.

Phase Two consists of four (4) epochs.
Each epoch is one (1) month.
Total Phase Two duration is four (4) months.

Phase Two participation is restricted to **Cerebral Paired Assets**, composed of:
- A Cerebral ERC-721 NFT, and
- Its corresponding Ethscription artifact.

Phase Two distributes a fixed total of **15,000,000 Brain Intelligence Token (BIT)**.
Rewards are settled once, at Phase Two close, after the end of Epoch 4.

Rewards are routed through ENS.
Eligibility is enforced using zero-knowledge (ZK) proofs.

Eligibility is binary.
A qualifying Cerebral Paired Asset receives full rewards or zero.
Partial rewards are not permitted.

Unused rewards return to the Rewards Reserve Treasury.

---

## Motivation

Phase Two requires strict, auditable, and non-custodial reward rules that remain
consistent with the protocol’s paired-asset evolution model.

This EIP:
- Applies the Phase One paired-asset eligibility model to Cerebrals
- Uses ENS as the canonical reward routing identity
- Requires continuous paired-asset staking
- Requires Proof of Care continuity
- Uses ZK proofs for verification
- Prevents inflation under all outcomes
- Clearly defines forfeiture and treasury reversion rules

---

## Scope

This EIP applies only to:
- Cerebral Paired Assets
- Phase Two rewards (4 epochs, 4 months)

This EIP does not define:
- Phase One rewards
- Rewards for Genesis Brains or Pickle Punks
- Phase Three rewards
- Token issuance mechanics
- Exchange or market behavior

## Definitions

**Phase Two**  
The second reward phase of the protocol.
Consists of four epochs.
Total duration is four months.

**Epoch**  
A one-month evaluation window within Phase Two.

**Cerebral Paired Asset**  
The canonical Phase Two participation unit composed of:
1. One Cerebral ERC-721 NFT, and  
2. Its corresponding Ethscription artifact.

Both components together are required for staking, Proof of Care evaluation,
and reward eligibility.

**Proof of Care Continuity**  
Protocol-defined continuity requirements validated through ZK proofs and
protocol checks.

**ENS Identity**  
A deterministic ENS subdomain under `bitbrains.eth` that resolves to the wallet
staking the Cerebral Paired Asset.

**Rewards Reserve Treasury**  
A dedicated treasury address used exclusively for future participant rewards.

---

## Eligibility Requirements (Normative)

To receive Phase Two rewards, a Cerebral Paired Asset MUST satisfy **all**
requirements for the entire Phase Two duration:

1. **Continuous Paired-Asset Staking**  
   - The Cerebral ERC-721 NFT MUST remain staked for all four epochs.  
   - The paired Ethscription artifact MUST remain concurrently valid and
     registered in protocol-defined good standing.  
   - Any unstaking, transfer, invalidation, or disassociation of either
     component permanently disqualifies the Paired Asset.

2. **Proof of Care Continuity**  
   Proof of Care continuity MUST remain unbroken for all four epochs.
   Continuity is validated using ZK proofs.

3. **ENS-Bound Ownership**  
   The ENS subdomain MUST resolve to the staking wallet.
   Ownership and resolution are validated via ZK proofs at Phase Two close.

Failure of any requirement results in zero rewards.

---

## Phase Two Reward Allocation (Normative)

Total Phase Two reward pool:
**15,000,000 BIT**

Total participating Cerebral Paired Assets:
**3,000**

Per-Cerebral Paired Asset reward allocation:

15,000,000 BIT ÷ 3,000  
= **5,000 BIT**

Rewards are distributed once, at Phase Two close.
No rewards are distributed per epoch.

---

## Disqualification and Forfeiture

Cerebral Paired Assets that fail eligibility requirements receive zero rewards.

Rewards allocated to disqualified or non-participating Paired Assets are not
redistributed to remaining participants.

---

## Treasury Reversion

Any portion of the Phase Two reward pool not distributed
MUST be returned to the Rewards Reserve Treasury.

Unused rewards MUST NOT be routed to the Team Treasury.
Funds remain reserved for future protocol rewards.

---

## Summary

Phase Two lasts four months across four epochs.

Only Cerebral Paired Assets participate.

100% paired-asset staking required.  
100% Proof of Care continuity required.

Reward outcome is binary:
**5,000 BIT or zero**.

Unused rewards return to the Rewards Reserve Treasury.
