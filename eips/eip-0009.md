## Abstract

This EIP defines eligibility, minting conditions, and reward rules for Cerebral NFTs
introduced in Phase 2 of the Bit Brains protocol.

Cerebrals are obtained exclusively through a Phase 2 public mint.

Genesis assets — including Genesis Brains and Pickle Punks (Ethscriptions) — do not
receive free, guaranteed, or entitlement-based Cerebrals. Ownership of Genesis assets
only confers eligibility to participate in the Phase 2 public mint under the same rules
as all participants.

This EIP specifies Phase 2 mint eligibility, staking and Proof of Care (PoC) enforcement,
Epoch 2 reward allocation, and enforcement via ENS-based identity and zero-knowledge
(ZK) verification.

---

## Scope

This EIP applies only to:
- Cerebral NFTs introduced in Phase 2
- Epoch 2 reward allocation

This EIP does not modify:
- Epoch 1 reward rules (EIP-0008)
- Token supply or emissions (EIP-0003)
- AIT execution constraints (EIP-0007)

---

## Definitions

- Cerebral  
  A Phase 2 ERC-721 NFT governed by this EIP.

- Genesis Asset  
  A protocol-recognized Genesis asset, including:
  - Genesis Brains
  - Pickle Punks (Ethscriptions)

- Epoch  
  A fixed reward accounting period.

- Staking  
  Protocol-defined staking state for a Cerebral.

- Proof of Care (PoC) Continuity  
  Protocol-defined continuity state required for reward eligibility.

- Secondary Rewards Treasury  
  A dedicated treasury used to hold forfeited and undistributed rewards, reserved
  exclusively for future participant incentives.

- ENS-Resolved Wallet  
  The wallet returned by resolving the participant’s canonical ENS identity.

---

## Phase 2 Cerebral Supply

Phase 2 introduces Cerebral NFTs through a single public mint.

- Total supply: 3,000 Cerebrals

Purpose:
- Onboard Phase 2 participants
- Expand protocol participation

All Cerebrals, regardless of minter identity or asset ownership, follow identical
staking, Proof of Care, and reward rules.

---

## Phase 2 Public Mint Eligibility

The Phase 2 public mint is open to:
- Genesis Brain holders
- Pickle Punk (Ethscription) holders
- New participants without Genesis assets

Eligibility requirements:
- Resolution of the protocol-defined ENS identity
- Successful zero-knowledge (ZK) eligibility verification
- Agreement to protocol staking and Proof of Care requirements

No participant receives guaranteed allocation, preferential quantity, or free mints.

---

## Staking and Proof of Care Enforcement

All Cerebrals:
- MUST be staked to be reward-eligible
- MUST maintain Proof of Care continuity
- MUST maintain valid ENS resolution
- MUST pass ZK verification checks

Failure to satisfy any requirement results in reward ineligibility.

---

## Exit and Forfeiture Rules

If a Cerebral holder:
- unstakes early,
- exits the protocol,
- breaks ENS resolution, or
- fails ZK eligibility checks,

then:
- pending rewards are forfeited.

---

## Forfeited Rewards

Forfeited rewards:
- SHALL remain within the fixed Epoch allocation
- SHALL be routed to the Secondary Rewards Treasury
- SHALL be reserved for future participant incentives

---

## Epoch 2 — Cerebral Reward Allocation

### Total Reward Pool

Epoch 2 allocates:
- 20,000,000 Brain Intelligence Token (BIT)

This allocation is hard-capped.
No discretionary minting is permitted.

---

### Distribution Conditions

Rewards are distributed only while:
- Cerebrals remain staked
- Proof of Care continuity is maintained
- ENS and ZK eligibility remain valid

---

### Compliance-Based Allocation

Per-Cerebral reward outcomes:

| Staking | PoC Continuity | Reward |
|--------|---------------|--------|
| true   | true          | 100%   |
| true   | false         | 50%    |
| false  | true          | 50%    |
| false  | false         | 0%     |

---

### Undistributed Rewards

Any rewards not distributed due to:
- partial compliance,
- non-compliance,
- forfeiture,

MUST:
- remain within the fixed Epoch 2 allocation,
- roll into the Secondary Rewards Treasury,
- be tagged for future rewards.

---

## Enforcement

Eligibility and reward claims are enforced exclusively via:
- ENS resolution
- zero-knowledge verification

No discretionary overrides are permitted.

---

## Non-Guarantee

- Reward rates are not fixed or perpetual
- Parameters may be reassessed post-AIT
- Total token supply SHALL NOT increase
- Accrued rewards SHALL NOT be modified retroactively

---

## Security and Economic Considerations

- Epoch 2 rewards are strictly capped at 20,000,000 BIT
- Accounting MUST be deterministic and auditable
- No reward dilution via preferential minting

---

## Conclusion

This EIP defines Phase 2 Cerebral minting and rewards as a single, uniform public system
that removes free entitlements, enforces Proof of Care and staking, preserves economic
discipline, and enables clean progression toward AIT states.
