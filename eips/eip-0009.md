## Abstract

This EIP defines eligibility, allocation, and reward rules for **Cerebral NFTs**
introduced in **Phase 2** of the Bit Brains protocol.

Cerebrals may be obtained through:
- **Genesis Participant entitlements** (Brains and Pickle Punks), and
- **Public Phase 2 minting**

This EIP specifies Phase 2 eligibility criteria, Cerebral mint entitlements,
Epoch 2 reward allocation, and enforcement via **ENS-based identity** and
**zero-knowledge (ZK) verification**.

---

## Scope

This EIP applies **only** to:
- Cerebral NFTs introduced in Phase 2
- Epoch 2 reward allocation

This EIP does **not** modify:
- Epoch 1 reward rules (EIP-0008)
- Token supply or emissions (EIP-0003)
- AIT execution constraints (EIP-0007)

---

## Definitions

- **Cerebral**  
  A Phase 2 ERC-721 NFT governed by this EIP.

- **Genesis Participant**  
  A protocol-recognized Genesis asset, including:
  - Genesis Brains, and
  - Pickle Punks (Ethscriptions).

- **Epoch**  
  A fixed reward accounting period.

- **Staking**  
  Protocol-defined staking state for a Cerebral or Genesis Participant.

- **Proof of Care (PoC) Continuity**  
  Protocol-defined continuity state required for full eligibility.

- **Secondary Rewards Treasury**  
  A dedicated treasury used to hold forfeited assets and undistributed rewards,
  reserved exclusively for future participant rewards.

- **ENS-Resolved Wallet**  
  The wallet returned by resolving the participant’s canonical ENS identity.

## Phase 2 Cerebral Supply

Phase 2 introduces Cerebral NFTs through two channels:

### 1. Genesis Entitlement Pool

Cerebrals reserved for Genesis Participants based on Phase 1 compliance.

### 2. Public Mint Pool

- **Total public supply:** **3,000 Cerebrals**
- Purpose:
  - Onboard new Phase 2 participants
  - Expand protocol participation

All Cerebrals, regardless of origin, follow identical staking and PoC rules.

---

## Genesis Participant Eligibility (Phase 2 Claims)

Genesis Participant eligibility is determined by **Phase 1 compliance**.

### Full Compliance (100%)

A Genesis Participant is 100% compliant if:
- the asset was staked, AND
- PoC continuity was valid.

Entitlement:
- eligible to claim **two (2) Cerebrals**.

### Partial Compliance (50%)

A Genesis Participant is 50% compliant if exactly one condition is satisfied:
- staking without PoC, OR
- PoC without staking.

Entitlement:
- eligible to claim **one (1) Cerebral**.

### Non-Compliance (0%)

If neither condition is satisfied:
- no Cerebral entitlement.

---

### Protocol-Owned Genesis Assets

The protocol controls **100 Genesis Brains**.

These assets:
- are staked,
- do not satisfy PoC continuity.

Entitlement:
- eligible to claim **one (1) Cerebral** per Brain.

Cerebrals claimed by the protocol:
- SHALL be staked under protocol control,
- SHALL follow identical reward rules,
- SHALL route rewards to the **Secondary Rewards Treasury**.

---

## Exit and Forfeiture Rules

If a Genesis Participant:
- exits the protocol,
- unstakes early,
- breaks ENS resolution, or
- fails ZK eligibility checks,

then:
- all unclaimed Cerebral entitlements are forfeited,
- all pending rewards are forfeited.

---

## Forfeited Cerebrals

Forfeited Cerebral NFTs:

- SHALL be routed to the **Secondary Rewards Treasury**
- SHALL remain staked under protocol control
- SHALL accrue rewards for future participant incentives

These assets:
- SHALL NOT default to the Team Treasury.

---

## Epoch 2 — Cerebral Reward Allocation

### Total Reward Pool

Epoch 2 allocates:

- **20,000,000 Brain Intelligence Token (BIT)** total

This allocation mirrors Epoch 1 in size and is hard-capped.

No discretionary minting is permitted.

---

### Distribution Conditions

Rewards are distributed only while:
- Cerebrals remain staked,
- PoC continuity is maintained,
- ENS and ZK eligibility remain valid.

---

### Compliance-Based Allocation

Per Cerebral reward outcomes:

| Staking | PoC Continuity | Reward |
|--------|---------------|--------|
| true   | true          | 100%   |
| true   | false         | 50%    |
| false  | true          | 50%    |
| false  | false         | 0%     |

---

### Undistributed Rewards

Any rewards not distributed due to:
- partial compliance,
- non-compliance,
- forfeiture,

MUST:
- remain within the fixed Epoch 2 allocation,
- roll into the **Secondary Rewards Treasury**,
- be tagged for future participant rewards.

---

## Public Cerebral Mint (Phase 2)

Publicly minted Cerebrals:
- MUST comply with the same staking rules,
- MUST comply with the same PoC rules,
- ARE subject to identical enforcement.

No public Cerebral receives preferential treatment.

---

## Enforcement

Eligibility and reward claims are enforced via:
- ENS resolution, and
- zero-knowledge verification.

No discretionary overrides are permitted.

---

## Non-Guarantee

- Reward rates are not fixed or perpetual.
- Parameters may be reassessed post-AIT.
- Total token supply SHALL NOT increase.
- Accrued rewards SHALL NOT be modified retroactively.

---

## Security and Economic Considerations

- Epoch 2 rewards are strictly capped at **20,000,000 BIT**
- Accounting MUST be deterministic and auditable
- No reward dilution via asset count expansion

---

## Conclusion

This EIP defines Phase 2 Cerebral eligibility and rewards in a manner that:
- honors Genesis participation (Brains and Pickle Punks),
- enforces continuity and staking,
- preserves economic discipline,
- and enables clean progression toward AIT states.
