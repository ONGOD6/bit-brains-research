## Abstract

This EIP defines the Phase Two reward distribution standard for Cerebrals
within the Bit Brains protocol.

Phase Two consists of four (4) epochs.
Each epoch is one (1) month.
Total Phase Two duration is four (4) months.

Cerebrals are ERC-721 NFTs.
They are the only participating assets in Phase Two.

Phase Two distributes a fixed total of 15,000,000 Brain Intelligence Token (BIT).
Rewards are settled once, at Phase Two close.
Settlement occurs after the end of Epoch 4.

Rewards are routed through ENS.
Eligibility is enforced using zero-knowledge (ZK) proofs.

Eligibility is binary.
A Cerebral receives full rewards or zero.
Partial rewards are not permitted.

Unused rewards return to the Rewards Reserve Treasury.

---

## Motivation

Phase Two requires strict, auditable, and non-custodial reward rules.

This EIP:
- Applies the EIP-0008 eligibility model to Cerebrals
- Uses ENS as the canonical reward routing identity
- Requires continuous staking
- Requires Proof of Care continuity
- Uses ZK proofs for verification
- Prevents inflation under all outcomes
- Defines forfeiture and treasury reversion rules

---

## Scope

This EIP applies only to:
- Cerebrals (ERC-721)
- Phase Two rewards (4 epochs, 4 months)

This EIP does not define:
- Phase One rewards
- Rewards for Genesis Brains or Pickle Punks
- Token issuance mechanics
- Exchange or market behavior

## Definitions

Phase Two  
The second reward phase of the protocol.
Consists of four epochs.
Total duration is four months.

Epoch  
A one-month evaluation window within Phase Two.

Cerebral  
A protocol-recognized ERC-721 NFT eligible for Phase Two rewards.

Proof of Care Continuity  
Protocol-defined continuity requirements.
Validated through ZK proofs and protocol checks.

ENS Identity  
A participant-chosen ENS subdomain under `bitbrains.eth`.
The chosen label may be the participant’s selected number.
The ENS name MUST resolve to the wallet staking the Cerebral.

Rewards Reserve Treasury  
A dedicated treasury address used exclusively for future participant rewards.

---

## Eligibility Requirements (Normative)

To receive Phase Two rewards, a Cerebral MUST satisfy all requirements
for the entire Phase Two duration:

1. Continuous Staking  
The Cerebral MUST remain staked for all four epochs.
Any unstaking, transfer, or sale permanently disqualifies the Cerebral.

2. Proof of Care Continuity  
Proof of Care continuity MUST remain unbroken.
Continuity is validated using ZK proofs.

3. ENS-Bound Ownership  
The ENS subdomain MUST resolve to the staking wallet.
Ownership and resolution are validated via ZK proofs.

Failure of any requirement results in zero rewards.

---

## Phase Two Reward Allocation (Normative)

Total Phase Two reward pool:
15,000,000 BIT

Total participating Cerebrals:
3,000

Per-Cerebral reward allocation:

15,000,000 BIT ÷ 3,000 Cerebrals
= 5,000 BIT

Rewards are distributed once.
Distribution occurs at Phase Two close.
No rewards are distributed per epoch.

---

## Disqualification and Forfeiture

Cerebrals that fail eligibility requirements receive zero rewards.

Rewards allocated to disqualified Cerebrals are not redistributed
to remaining participants.

---

## Treasury Reversion

Any portion of the Phase Two reward pool not distributed
MUST be returned to the Rewards Reserve Treasury.

Unused rewards MUST NOT be routed to the Team Treasury.
Funds remain reserved for future protocol rewards.
