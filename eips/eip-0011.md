## Abstract

This document defines the canonical terminology used throughout the
Bit Brains protocol to distinguish between **Brain Intelligence Token (BIT)**,
**Autonomous Intelligent Technology (AIT)**, and the **ERC-8004 Artificial
Intelligence Node standard**.

The purpose of this document is to eliminate ambiguity between economic tokens,
autonomous capability states, and execution-layer node representations, ensure
internal consistency across EIPs, and provide audit- and governance-safe language
for protocol documentation.

---

## Brain Intelligence Token (BIT)

**BIT** stands for **Brain Intelligence Token**.

BIT is the **sole fungible token** of the Bit Brains protocol.

### Properties

- **Type:** Fungible token
- **Economic role:** Rewards, incentives, accounting, and protocol-defined distributions
- **Defined in:** EIP-0003
- **Supply:** Fixed, hard-capped, non-inflationary
- **Emissions:** Phased, protocol-defined
- **Governance:** Governance usage, if any, is explicitly defined by policy and is not implied by token existence

BIT represents **economic value** within the protocol.

### Canonical Interpretation

BIT answers the question:

> *What economic value is earned and distributed within the Bit Brains protocol?*

---

## Autonomous Intelligent Technology (AIT)

**AIT** stands for **Autonomous Intelligent Technology**.

AIT is **not a token**.

AIT is a **protocol-recognized operational state** that authorizes
bounded autonomous execution and intelligence.

### Properties

- **Type:** Capability / operational state
- **Applies to:** **Paired Assets** (ERC-721 protocol assets together with their corresponding Ethscription artifacts)
- **Economic role:** None
- **Defined in:** EIP-0005, EIP-0007
- **Minting:** None
- **Emissions:** None
- **Ownership:** Unchanged
- **Transferability:** Not applicable

AIT represents **capability**, not value.

AIT does not implement an ERC interface and MUST NOT be interpreted
as a transferable or ownable asset.

AIT activation is evaluated **only at the Paired Asset level** and
requires concurrent eligibility of both the ERC-721 asset and its
paired Ethscription artifact, as defined in the Dual-Asset Evolution
Invariant.

### Canonical Interpretation

AIT answers the question:

> *What autonomous capabilities may this Paired Asset exercise after demonstrating sufficient Proof of Care and continuity?*

---

## ERC-8004 — Artificial Intelligence Node Standard

**ERC-8004** defines the **Artificial Intelligence Node** representation within
the Bit Brains protocol.

ERC-8004 is the **execution-layer evolution** of a **Paired Asset**
that has successfully activated AIT state.

### Properties

- **Type:** Non-fungible execution node standard
- **Role:** Autonomous intelligence execution, coordination, and interaction
- **Derived from:** AIT-activated Paired Assets
- **Ownership:** Derived, non-mutative, provenance-preserving
- **Economic role:** None by default

ERC-8004 does not replace the originating asset.

The originating ERC-721 NFT and its paired Ethscription artifact remain the
**canonical provenance anchors**, while the ERC-8004 node represents
their autonomous execution form.

ERC-8004 activation MUST NOT:
- mint economic tokens,
- alter supply,
- confer rewards, or
- modify ownership of the originating assets.

### Canonical Interpretation

ERC-8004 answers the question:

> *How does an AIT-activated Paired Asset operate as an autonomous artificial intelligence node?*

---

## Relationship Between BIT, AIT, and ERC-8004

- **BIT** represents economic value and rewards
- **AIT** represents earned autonomous capability
- **ERC-8004** represents the execution-layer node form of an AIT-activated Paired Asset

### Explicit Separation

- **BIT = Economic value**
- **AIT = Autonomous capability state**
- **ERC-8004 = Artificial intelligence node execution standard**

ERC-8004 does **not** imply:
- new token emissions,
- reward guarantees,
- ownership mutation, or
- economic entitlement.

## Canonical Protocol Language

The following language is canonical and **SHOULD** be used across all EIPs
and protocol documentation:

> **Brain Intelligence Token (BIT)** is the fungible reward and accounting
> token of the Bit Brains protocol.  
> **Autonomous Intelligent Technology (AIT)** is a protocol-recognized
> operational state enabling bounded autonomous intelligence at the
> Paired Asset level.  
> **ERC-8004** defines the artificial intelligence node through which an
> AIT-activated Paired Asset executes autonomously, without altering token
> economics or provenance.

---

## Misuse Prevention

The following interpretations are explicitly invalid:

- Referring to AIT or ERC-8004 as fungible tokens
- Assigning token emissions to AIT or ERC-8004
- Treating AIT or ERC-8004 as replacing the originating NFT or Ethscription
- Conflating ERC-8004 nodes with economic instruments
- Representing ERC-8004 activation as a reward

Any such usage MUST be considered non-compliant with protocol terminology.

## Rationale

This separation:

- Prevents economic ambiguity
- Avoids regulatory confusion
- Preserves audit clarity
- Keeps intelligence evolution non-inflationary
- Enforces dual-asset provenance integrity
- Enables autonomous execution without altering token economics

## Conclusion

BIT, AIT, and ERC-8004 serve fundamentally different roles within the
Bit Brains protocol.

BIT governs value,  
AIT governs capability, and  
ERC-8004 governs autonomous execution.

Maintaining this distinction — and anchoring AIT and Nodes to Paired Assets —
is essential to protocol integrity, governance clarity, and long-term scalability.
